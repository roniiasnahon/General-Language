About Cosmiwise

Cosmiwise is an AI-powered assistant and research companion designed to orchestrate computing workspaces seamlessly. It helps users build future-proof skills by integrating directly into their daily workflows, offering intelligent suggestions, data parsing, and context-aware responses.

Key Features

  • Agentic Research: Seamlessly gather, organize, and synthesize information from multiple sources.
  • Workspace Orchestration: Integrate with your existing tools and manage your digital environment efficiently.
  • Future-Proof Learning: Designed to adapt and grow with your educational and professional needs.

Available Models

Cosmiwise features multiple advanced AI models including Flamenca, Ericka V, and Triolett II, tailored to different research and reasoning tasks.
